## One-Line Intro

I build practical AI systems with operational teams: agents, apps, and workflows that remove repetitive work and help teams move faster.

## Short Bio

Andres Santos Sanz is Applied AI Lead at Bit2Me, where he co-creates agents, apps, and workflows with domain experts to accelerate internal teams. Before Bit2Me, he spent four years at Revolut across Growth Analytics and CX Operations, working on internal data products, referral growth, support operations, onboarding, offboarding, and restricted-user processes. He previously worked in logistics and industrial operations at Amazon, Zennio, and IMTO.

His background sits at the intersection of data, process improvement, product, and operations. He is strong in SQL, Python, internal tooling, automation, and AI-assisted coding agents.
